Who I am

Interaction Designer and Programmer

Born in Sweden and raised on the Canary Islands (Spain) in a three language environment (Swedish, Spanish and English), already from a young age I had to adapt to a different culture from my own, which expanded my curiosity and an open mind. This leads me to pursue goals outside of my comfort zone, explore and always want to try and learn many things.

At the age of 18, I moved back to Sweden to pursue higher education as a Game Developer at Uppsala University Campus Gotland. During my education, I was lucky to partake in an exchange program to South Korea, where I found my passion for Visualization and Interaction Design, as well as pursue my fourth language. After graduation, I continued my studies in a Master's Program in Interactive Media Technology at KTH.
